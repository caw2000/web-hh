Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Honor Homes ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our text messaging services (the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Services, including:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact Information: When you register for an account (if applicable) or contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.
  • Message Content: We collect the content of the text messages you send and receive through our Services.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our Services, such as the date, time, and recipients of your messages.
  • Payment Information: If you use our Services for paid services, we collect payment information, such as your credit card number or other payment details.

Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including the device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Log Information: We automatically collect certain information in our server logs, including your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and timestamps.
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect information about your device's location.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and other similar technologies to collect information about your use of our Services and to personalize your experience.

Information from Third-Party Sources:

  • We may receive information about you from third-party sources, such as service providers, partners, and public sources. For example, if you access our Services through a third-party platform, we may receive information about you from that platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide and Improve Our Services: To deliver text messages, operate our platform, and enhance the functionality and user experience of our Services.
  • Communicate with You: To send you transactional messages, updates, and marketing communications (where permitted and according to your preferences).
  • Personalize Your Experience: To customize the content and features of our Services based on your preferences and usage patterns.
  • Process Payments: To process transactions and prevent fraud (if applicable).
  • Analyze Usage: To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Services.
  • Protect Our Rights and Safety: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud and other illegal activities, and to protect our rights and the rights of our users.
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • Research and Development: To improve our services.
  • With Consent: We may use your information for other purposes with your consent.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, payment processing, analytics, and customer support. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Mobile Carriers: We may share your information with mobile carriers as necessary to transmit text messages.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or government requests.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose your information if we believe it is necessary to protect our rights, property, and safety, or the rights of others.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties with your consent.
  • Aggregated and Anonymized Information: We may share aggregated and anonymized information that does not directly identify you with third parties for analytics and marketing.
  • SMS Opt-In Data: No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Your Choices

  • Access and Correction: You may have the right to access and correct inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You may opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications or by contacting us directly.
  • Location Information: You can control the collection of your device's location information through your device settings.
  • Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings and other tools.
  • Do Not Track: Because there is no industry consensus on how to respond to DNT signals, we do not currently respond to DNT signals.
  • Data Retention: We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age without parental involvement. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, please be aware that we transfer and process personal information in the United States.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website or through other reasonable means. Your continued use of our Services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
